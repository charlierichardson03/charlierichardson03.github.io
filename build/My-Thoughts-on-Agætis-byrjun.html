My Thoughts on Agætis byrjun

Agætis byrjun combines transcendent falsetto vocals and ambient guitar noises to craft a masterpiece.

review
Charlie Richardson
11/5/2020

I really like Agætis byrjun by Sigur Rós. My favorite tracks were Svefn-g-englar, Starálfur, and Viorar vel til tloftárása. I thought the vocals throughout the project were fantastic, but one of the project's defining features is the use of bowed guitars. Sometimes the bowed guitars act as a melody, and at other times they're just a texture, but they add something to every track they're on.

I would probably give this album an 8.5/10 overall, because I thought some of the tracks were too similar to each other. I suggest everyone listen to this album, and I'm happy that Mr. Gohde recommended this album.
