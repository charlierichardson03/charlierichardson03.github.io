Quick Question About Post-Rock and Pink Floyd

I'm not sure that this matters but I'm just curious.

conceptual question
Charlie Richardson
11/6/2020

It seems pretty clear to me that a lot of the more transcendent, mellow post-rock groups are inspired by Pink Floyd. Songs exceeding the 15 minute mark, wacky guitar sounds, untraditional song structure, etc. Does anyone know if Pink Floyd is categorized as Post-Rock? I know they're from a completely different era but some of their music fits into the genre so well.

I guess the same question could be asked for some Velvet Underground albums, although Velvet Underground would definitely be on the more abrasive side of Post-Rock. What I'm getting it here is that I'm not sure how we should categorize albums from different eras that fit so seamlessly into the genre. I guess genres are arbitrary anyway though and music is just music so who cares.
