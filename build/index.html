Post-Rock Live Performance

This is a live performance in London in 2010 from "Godspeed You! Black Emperor," a group that rose to prominence in the mid 90's. Their songs are usually instrumental and often surpass the 20-minute mark. In this recording, they perform the song "East Hastings" from their debut album, "F#A#∞".

A Critically Acclaimed Post-Rock Project

"Long Season" is a 35-minute one-song album by "Fishmans," a Japanese group that was active throughout the late 20th century. The song was written by Shinji Sato, who passed away around three years after the song was released.
